About me

I’m Dr. Hani Zahran, Vice Chairman of the Saudi Society for Geoscience and Manager of the Disaster Center at the Saudi Geological Survey. With over 24 years in the geoscience field, I focus on managing and studying natural hazards such as earthquakes and volcanoes. I am a seasoned Geophysicist and Seismologist with extensive experience across governmental and research sectors. As a Consultant for Earthquakes and Volcanoes at the Saudi Geological Survey since April 2019, and previously as General Manager of the National Center for Earthquake and Volcanoes from 2005 to 2019, I have led significant projects on national safety and seismic research.
